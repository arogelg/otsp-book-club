Our Story

The On The Same Page Book Club began as a small gathering of friends who loved getting lost in stories. Over time, our circle has grown into a welcoming community of readers from all walks of life. Whether you're into fantasy epics, thoughtful nonfiction, or anything in between, you'll find someone here who shares your passions.

Meeting Schedule

We gather weekly on Friday or Saturday evenings depending on everyone's availability. Meetings are laid‑back and flexible—join when you can and share as much or as little as you like.
